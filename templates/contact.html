Guidelines for creating the best password:

  • Be at least 12 characters long but 14 or more is generally better.
  • Include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers and special characters.
  • Avoid using common words such as names, birthdates or common words found in dictionaries.
  • Use a combination of random characters to make the password less predictable.
  • Avoid password information such as your username, email address, name or any publicly available about yourself.
  • Avoid common patterns for example “123456” or “qwerty”.
